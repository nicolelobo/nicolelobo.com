Web | Mockup | Graphics

Album Cover Art: X

HOST

Sears DX Canadian High School Design Competition

TIMEFRAME

May 2015

PROJECT DESCRIPTION

This is my re-design of Ed Sheeran's album cover "X".

Insprired by Ed's warm acoustic renditions and firey red hair, I chose the colour theme of orange and brown, which is a stark contrast to the original green. The songs tell dificult and emotional stories from his life, which is why the X's are floating dizzingly close his head. The most important part are the lyrics- they were without doubt written and re-written numerous times to get them perfect, which is why I chose a crumpled paper backgorund.
This is what the cover would look like on music download platform, as well as on the screen of a moblie device. The design is simple and versatile- it looks great from a large CD cover to a small moblie screen.
